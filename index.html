About Me

"Nobody Trips over Mountains.
It is the Small Pebble that Causes you to Stumble."

My name is Xiaofeng Zhou, an undergraduate student in Southeast University, China.
I entered Southeast University in August, 2016, and is expected to receive my bachelor degree in information science and technology in June, 2020.
My current research focus on the FPGA/ASIC implementation for polar codes and polar decoder algorithms, including the VLSI architecture design for polar decoder and performance optimization for polar decoder algorithms.
Currently looking for Ph.D positions in these areas.

Research Interests:

FPGA/ASIC Baed Digital Front-End Design, Polar Codes, Implementation of NN Algorithms
